## Data Processing Pipeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Ingestion">
    ALINA ingests data from multiple sources including social media, news outlets, and blockchain networks, etc...
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Cleaning and Preprocessing">
    Raw data is cleaned, normalized, and prepared for analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Feature Extraction">
    Relevant features are extracted from the preprocessed data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model Application">
    Machine learning models are applied to the extracted features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insight Generation">
    The results are synthesized into actionable insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
