# Core Philosophy: The Power of Attention Flow

Unlike traditional finance, where information flows through public channels, DeFi/Web3 information primarily moves through closed networks (social sites, private chats, etc.).

<Card title="Our Guiding Principle" icon="lightbulb">
  **Where attention goes, money flows.** By tracking real-time market chatter and combining it with money flow data, we can identify where the next trend will emerge.
</Card>

## The Information Challenge

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Decentralized Tech vs. Centralized Info" icon="network-wired">
    Despite blockchain's transparency, crucial market-moving information remains in closed channels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transaction vs. Context" icon="magnifying-glass">
    On-chain data shows what happened, but not why - missing the critical context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Our Approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Direct Data Collection">
    Source information directly from individuals and organizations at the source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-time Analysis">
    Process market chatter and track narrative shifts as they happen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flow Correlation">
    Monitor where money flows in relation to attention shifts, not just limited to event triggers of social events but onchain events as well.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Predictive Insights">
    Identify emerging opportunities before they become mainstream across the onchain and offchain realms.
  </Step>
</Steps>
