ALINA (Algorithmic Learning and Integrated Network Analytics) is the advanced AI system that powers Kairos Terminal. It's designed to process vast amounts of data from various sources to provide actionable insights for crypto traders.

Long story short, it connects all the scattered dots which might be imperceptible to human eye
