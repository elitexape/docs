## Interpreting Sentiment Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bullish Sentiment (0.6 to 1.0)">
    Indicates a strongly positive market outlook. Often correlates with price increases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Neutral Sentiment (-0.2 to 0.2)">
    Suggests market uncertainty or balance between positive and negative sentiments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bearish Sentiment (-1.0 to -0.6)">
    Indicates a strongly negative market outlook. Often correlates with price decreases.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  While sentiment analysis is a powerful tool, it should be used in conjunction with other analytical methods for the most informed trading decisions.
</Warning>
