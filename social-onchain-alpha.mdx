# Social-Onchain Alpha

Social-Onchain Alpha is a unique feature that correlates social media trends with on-chain activities, providing you with deeper insights into market movements and potential opportunities.

<Card title="Bridging Social and On-Chain Data" icon="bridge">

</Card>
